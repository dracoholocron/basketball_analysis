# Quickstart — Get Running in 5 Minutes

## What you need
- A computer or tablet with a modern browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
- Your login email and password (provided by your admin or IT staff)

## Step 1: Open the app

Navigate to your team's Basketball IQ URL in your browser. It looks like:
```
https://basketball-iq.yourteam.com
```
or locally during setup:
```
http://localhost:4000
```

## Step 2: Sign in

1. Enter your **email address**
2. Enter your **password**
3. Click **Sign In**

![Login screen](./screenshots/01-login.png)

You will land on the **Dashboard**.

## Step 3: Create your first matchup

1. Click **Game Day** in the left sidebar
2. Click **New Matchup** in the top right
3. Give it a name (e.g. "vs. Eagles — Dec 20")
4. Select your team and the opponent
5. Click **Create**

## Step 4: Run a simulation

1. Still on Game Day, click on the matchup you just created
2. Click **Run Simulation (1,000 games)**
3. Wait ~5 seconds — you'll see a win probability percentage

## Step 5: Go live on game night

1. Click **Game Tracker** in the sidebar
2. Select your matchup from the dropdown
3. Click anywhere on the court diagram to log a shot or play event

You're ready! Explore each module using the Table of Contents in the [README](./README.md).

## Tips
- Use **Coach Mode** (top-right button) to simplify all numbers into plain language
- The **Matchup Workspace** ties everything together in one place

## Common pitfalls
- "I can't log in" → Check your email spelling; passwords are case-sensitive
- "I don't see my team" → Ask your admin to create the team in the Admin section first
