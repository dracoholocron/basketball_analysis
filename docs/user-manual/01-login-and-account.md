# Login & Account

## What is this

Your account gives you access to your team's Basketball IQ platform. Access is role-based:
- **Admin**: Can manage all teams, players, seasons, and users
- **Coach**: Full access to scouting, game tracking, simulation, and plays

## How to sign in

1. Navigate to your Basketball IQ URL
2. Enter your **email** and **password**
3. Click **Sign In**

If this is your first time, contact your team admin to create your account.

## Sign out

Click **Sign Out** at the very bottom of the left sidebar.

## Tips
- Your session stays active for 24 hours; you'll be prompted to log in again after that
- Your Coach Mode preference and other UI settings are saved per browser
