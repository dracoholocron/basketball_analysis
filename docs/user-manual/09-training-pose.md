# Training & Pose Analysis

## What is this

Upload practice video clips to get AI-powered analysis of individual player shooting mechanics. The system detects body joints and calculates angles at key moments of the shooting motion.

## How to open it

Click **Training** in the sidebar, or navigate to `/training`.

![Training sessions list](./screenshots/23-training-list.png)

## Step by step

### 1. Create a Session

Click **New Session** → enter a drill name (e.g. "Marcus Free Throw — Week 3") → Create.

### 2. Upload Video

Find your session in the list → click **Upload Video** → select your file → wait for upload.

### 3. Run Analysis

Click on the session to open it → click **Run Pose Analysis** → wait 2-5 minutes.

### 4. Review Results

**Video Overlay**: Watch the skeleton or bounding box overlays synchronized to the video.

**Joint Angle HUD**: Average angles for:
- Elbow L/R (release arm bend)
- Knee L/R (jump depth)
- Hip L/R (lateral alignment)
- Torso Lean (forward bend)
- Release Angle (ball trajectory angle)

**Frame Table**: Exact metrics per frame for detailed review.

![Skeleton overlay](./screenshots/24-training-skeleton-overlay.png)

## Tips
- Focus on release angle (ideally 45-55°) and elbow alignment for free throw improvement
- Film multiple sessions to track improvement over time
- Keep video clips to 30-90 seconds for fastest analysis
