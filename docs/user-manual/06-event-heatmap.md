# Event Heatmap

## What is this

The Event Heatmap shows a visual breakdown of where shots were taken during a tracked game. It also shows aggregate stats (FG%, blocks, steals, fouls) and the live progress of your Keys to Victory.

## How to open it

From the Game Tracker, click **Event Heatmap** in the toolbar. Or navigate to `/game-tracker/[matchup-id]/event-heatmap`.

![Event Heatmap](./screenshots/15-event-heatmap.png)

## What you see

### Shot Heatmap
The court is divided into zones. Darker red = more shots in that zone. Click anywhere to navigate back.

### Aggregate Stats
- **FG%** — field goal percentage for your team
- **Blocks** — total blocks
- **Steals** — total steals
- **Fouls** — total fouls

### Keys to Victory Progress
Each Key to Victory shows a **TRACKING** or **AT RISK** badge plus a progress bar for each metric target.

![Per-player progress](./screenshots/16-heatmap-per-player-progress.png)

## Tips
- Use this after the game to identify shot selection patterns
- The heatmap updates in real time if you're viewing it during a live game
