# Dashboard

## What is this

The Dashboard is your weekly hub. It shows your upcoming matchups, quick access to all modules, and the status of your most recent video analysis jobs.

The **Weekly Rhythm widget** is the heart of the dashboard: it shows each upcoming matchup with a 5-step prep progress tracker so you know at a glance what's ready and what still needs work.

## How to open it

Click **Dashboard** (house icon) in the top of the left sidebar, or navigate to `/`.

![Dashboard](./screenshots/02-dashboard.png)

## Step by step

### 1. Review Upcoming Matchups

Each matchup card shows:
- Opponent name and game date
- **Win Probability** (from the last simulation run)
- **5-step stepper**: Scouting → Simulation → Game Plan → Plays → Tracker

Steps light up green as you complete them. A progress bar at the bottom shows overall readiness percentage.

### 2. Quick Actions

Four tiles give you one-click access to the most common tasks:
- **New Game** — record a game and upload video
- **Scout Opponent** — generate an AI scouting report
- **Game Day Prep** — run simulations and set Keys to Victory
- **Matchup Workspace** — the all-in-one pre-game hub

### 3. Recent Analysis Jobs

The bottom section lists your 6 most recent video analysis jobs with their status (pending, running, done, failed).

## Tips
- The stepper only updates when you complete each step — run a simulation to advance "Step 2: Simulation"
- Click any matchup card to jump straight to its Matchup Workspace

## Common pitfalls
- "I don't see any upcoming matchups" → Create a matchup in Game Day and set a future `scheduled_at` date
- "Win probability shows 50%" → Run a simulation first
