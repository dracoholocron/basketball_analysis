# Scouting Reports

## What is this

Scouting Reports are AI-generated analyses of your opponent based on their uploaded box scores. The report highlights tendencies, strengths, weaknesses, and player-by-player notes.

## How to open it

Click **Scouting** in the sidebar, or navigate to `/scouting`.

## Step by step

### 1. Select a Matchup

Choose from the matchup dropdown at the top.

### 2. Generate the Report

Click **Generate Report with AI**. This calls the LLM (AI language model) and takes about 10-30 seconds.

### 3. Review the Report

The report includes:
- **TL;DR** — 2-3 sentence summary
- **Key Tendencies** — how this opponent plays
- **Player Spotlights** — top performers to watch
- **Vulnerabilities** — areas to attack

### 4. Add Coach Notes

The report has a free-text **Coach Notes** field. Type your own observations — these are saved automatically.

### 5. Regenerate

Click **Re-generate** (with the force option) if you've added new box scores and want a fresh analysis.

## Tips
- The more box scores you upload, the more accurate the scouting report
- Use the Matchup Workspace "Scouting" tab as a quick gateway to this page
