# Scenario: Weekly Prep (Monday → Friday Game)

**Audience**: Head coach or assistant coach  
**Time required**: 30-45 minutes spread across the week  
**Goal**: Arrive at game night with a complete, data-driven game plan

---

## Monday: Set Up the Matchup

1. Log in → Dashboard
2. Click **Game Day** in the sidebar
3. Click **New Matchup** → name it "vs. [Opponent] — [Date]"
4. Select your team and the opponent → Click **Create**
5. Go to **Admin → Box Scores** → import the opponent's last 5 box scores as a CSV
6. Return to **Game Day** → click your new matchup → click **Run Simulation**

You now have a baseline win probability.

---

## Tuesday: Scouting Report

1. Go to **Scouting** in the sidebar
2. Select your matchup
3. Click **Generate Report with AI** → wait ~15 seconds
4. Review the **TL;DR** section and the top opponent tendencies
5. Add your own notes in the **Coach Notes** field

---

## Wednesday: Game Plan

1. Go to **Game Day** → select your matchup
2. Review the **Keys to Victory** cards — identify which ones align with your game plan
3. Click the **📌 pin icon** on the 3 most important keys
4. The **Top 3 Priorities** panel now shows them in ranked order
5. Go to **Game Plan Notes** → write your:
   - Rotation Plan (starter lineup, when to sub)
   - Defensive Keys (who to focus on)
   - Strategic Playbook (primary play sets)

---

## Thursday: Build Plays

1. Go to **Play Builder** in the sidebar
2. Click a play from the library or create a new one
3. Filter the library by **Tag = "vs Zone"** or **Pace = "half-court"** to find relevant plays
4. In the canvas, use the **Add Frame** button to diagram the counter action
5. When done, save and link the play to your matchup

---

## Friday: Final Check via Matchup Workspace

1. Go to **Matchup Workspace** → click your matchup
2. Check the **Overview tab** — all 5 steps should be partially or fully green
3. Review **Top 3 Priorities** — confirm they match what you've been practicing
4. Click the **Notes tab** → do a final read-through of your game plan
5. Optionally enable **Coach Mode** and project the workspace for your team

**You're ready for tip-off.**

---

## Tips
- If win probability changes significantly after adding box scores, re-run the simulation
- The matchup workspace is mobile-friendly — you can review it courtside on a tablet
